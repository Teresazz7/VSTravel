Get To Know Us

About VSTravel

VSTravel is one of Indonesia's leading travel agencies, offering carefully curated and high-quality travel experiences. Established in Jakarta in 2020, we began as a small local travel agent and have grown into a trusted name with four active branches—two in Jakarta, one in Medan, and one in Surabaya.

We specialize in domestic travel packages, tailored tours, group and corporate trips, and personalized travel consultation services. Every journey we design is crafted with care and passion, aiming to deliver not just vacations, but unforgettable memories for every traveler. Our mission is to provide exceptional travel services with integrity and attention to detail, while our vision is to become the most trusted and innovative travel partner in Southeast Asia. With a loyal customer base and a commitment to excellence, we are now expanding our reach through a digital platform—connecting even more travelers to the beauty of Indonesia.

Our Branches

We currently operate from our Head Office in Jakarta, with additional branches in Jakarta, Medan, and Surabaya. Each branch is ready to serve and support your travel plans with personalized assistance.

Our offices are strategically located to ensure we can provide excellent service to clients from different regions. Every location is equipped with a dedicated team of travel experts who can assist you with planning your perfect journey, whether for leisure, business, or special occasions.

Visit any of our branches to discuss your travel plans in person, or contact us through our digital platforms for remote assistance. We pride ourselves on creating personalized experiences that meet the unique needs of each traveler.

VSTravel History

Since our founding in 2020, VSTravel has expanded rapidly thanks to our dedication to service and quality. What began as a small office in Jakarta has now grown to include multiple branches and a loyal customer base. In 2025, we're taking a major step forward by launching our digital platform — a website that helps us connect with travelers across Indonesia and beyond. This marks a new chapter as we aim to make Indonesian travel experiences accessible to a global audience.
